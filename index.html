Who We Are

Behavioral health expertise, built into every engagement

EMLS Consulting is a behavioral health systems consultancy dedicated to transforming the infrastructure of mental health and substance use disorder treatment. Founded by a Licensed Mental Health Counselor and Certified Addiction Professional with over 25 years of experience, we guide organizations from concept through licensure and launch.

Our focus is ethical practice, clinical excellence, and sustainable systems of care — building treatment environments that strengthen communities and elevate standards across the behavioral health continuum.
