Meet Your Therapist

Sergio Muriel

LMHC · CAP · QS · ARISE Intervention

Sergio Muriel is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor, Certified Addictions Professional, ARISE Intervention-trained clinician, and Qualified Supervisor with over 25 years of experience in the behavioral health field. He is recognized for his expertise in mental health and addiction treatment, with a strong focus on helping individuals and families understand and heal from maladaptive patterns developed in childhood.

Sergio specializes in working through dysfunctional family systems, guiding clients to build insight into survival-based behaviors that may have once been necessary but are now negatively impacting their relationships and overall well-being. His approach is rooted in increasing awareness, fostering accountability, and helping clients reconnect the past to present-day patterns in a way that promotes meaningful and lasting change.

Through a combination of clinical expertise, direct communication, and compassionate support, Sergio helps individuals and families develop healthier coping strategies, improve emotional regulation, and break cycles of dysfunction. He works collaboratively with clients to strengthen relationships, establish healthier boundaries, and create new, sustainable ways of relating to themselves and others.

Sergio’s work is grounded in the belief that with the right guidance, insight, and tools, individuals and families can move beyond survival patterns and build more authentic, stable, and fulfilling connections.

Common Questions

Are sessions in-person or telehealth?

All sessions are conducted via secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth video — so you can attend from the privacy and comfort of your own home. Telehealth is available to residents anywhere in Florida and New Jersey.

What happens during a free consultation?

It's a short, no-pressure phone conversation — usually 15 minutes. You'll share what brought you in, ask any questions you have, and decide together whether it feels like a good fit. There's no obligation to book after.

How long are sessions and how often do we meet?

Standard individual sessions are 50 minutes. Most clients begin weekly, then taper to every other week or monthly as progress is made. We'll find a cadence that fits your life and your goals.

Do you offer couples and family therapy?

Yes. Sergio works with couples and families in addition to individual clients. Couples and family sessions are conducted via telehealth and can be discussed during your free consultation.

Is what I share in therapy confidential?

Yes. Everything discussed is confidential and protected by law, with a few legally required exceptions (imminent danger to self or others, suspected abuse of a minor or vulnerable adult). These limits are explained fully at your first session.

What if I'm in crisis right now?

This practice is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, please call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or call 911 right away.

In a crisis? This site is not for emergencies. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call or text 988 or call 911.
